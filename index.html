Meet Dolev

A chess coach with over 10 years of experience teaching students of all ages and levels. Dolev has taught private lessons, group classes, and school programs, and has played at leading clubs such as Haifa Chess Club and Los Angeles Chess Club.

With a US rating of around 1850 and a Chess.com rating of 2200, he combines strong chess knowledge with excellent teaching skills. Dolev has taught over 100 students and is known for creating engaging, personalized lessons, in both English and Hebrew.
