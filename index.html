Meet Dolev

Chess is more than a game — it's a tradition of passing wisdom from one generation to the next. Dolev brings years of competitive and teaching experience to every session, creating an environment where learning feels like gathering around a table with friends.

With a passion for making chess accessible and enjoyable, Dolev specializes in small group instruction that builds not just better players, but sharper thinkers. Every lesson is crafted to challenge and inspire, whether you're picking up your first pawn or preparing for tournament play.
